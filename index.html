What is DUaid?

DUaid is a dedicated platform run by student volunteers, focused on everything that revolves around the University of Delhi. From timely updates and news to essential academic resources like study materials, syllabi, and previous year question papers, we strive to make the DU experience seamless and accessible for all. Our resources cater to both English and Hindi medium students, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility for all, including those facing physical challenges.

"Delhi University has consistently been at the forefront of academic innovation, offering a rich environment where critical thinking is encouraged, and diverse perspectives are nurtured. It remains a cornerstone of intellectual growth in India, shaping not only scholars but also future leaders."

Prof. Romila Thapar, History and Beyond

Our Vision

At DUaid, we aspire to create a vibrant community where every student at the University of Delhi stays informed, empowered, and supported in their academic journey. By bridging the information gap, we aim to ensure that no student is left behind in the fast-paced academic environment of DU.
