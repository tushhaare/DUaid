DU Extends Exam Form Deadline: Dec 20



The notification from the University of Delhi, dated December 9, 2024, informs Regular and NCWEB students of Semester I under the NEP-UGCF-2022 Scheme about the extended deadline for submitting examination forms for the academic session 2024-2025. Students can now submit their forms without a late fee until 20th December 2024, by 11:55 PM. This applies to all concerned students, including essential repeaters, improvement candidates, and ex-students of Semester I.

Key Details from the Notification:

Students must fill in their basic details, select their program, and enter their name as it appears on their X and XII class certificates. They will use their enrollment number and follow OTP verification to register on the provided portal: https://slc.uod.ac.in.

After registration, students are required to pay the examination fee online. If a student has already paid this fee to their Faculty/Department/College, they can request a refund for the duplicate payment.

After submitting the form, students should keep a printout of the verified examination form for future reference. Admit cards will be issued after the form is confirmed by the respective Faculty/Department/College.

Separate provisions will be made for students of the School of Open Learning (SOL). They are advised to contact their respective institution for specific instructions.

Students are encouraged to complete the process well before the deadline to avoid technical issues or last-minute discrepancies. Regular checks on the official portal are recommended for updates or clarifications.
