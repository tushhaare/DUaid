About us

Our Initiative

DUaid is a platform started in November 2024 by Tushar, a second year student pursuing B.A. (Hons.) History at A.R.S.D. College, this platform is dedicated to empowering students of Delhi University by offering comprehensive resources and support to make their academic journey smoother and more successful. Whether you are enrolled in Regular courses, SOL, or NCWEB, DUaid ensures you have access to everything you need to excel, from high-quality study materials to essential updates about university life.

We understand the challenges students face in a competitive environment like Delhi University, where accessing the right resources can often feel overwhelming. At DUaid, our mission is to bridge these gaps and make education more inclusive and accessible for all. Whether it's finding brokerage-free accommodation options directly from property owners or providing expertly curated notes, books, and previous year question papers, DUaid is designed to be a reliable companion for every DU student.

Our platform is driven by a spirit of collaboration and empathy. It is run by a dedicated team of student volunteers who work tirelessly to ensure timely and relevant content is available to help others succeed. We take pride in offering resources that cater to a diverse range of students, including those who prefer Hindi-medium materials or those with physical challenges like visual impairments. By providing materials in both English and Hindi, we aim to eliminate language barriers and make learning accessible to everyone.

Beyond academics, DUaid also fosters a vibrant community of students, creating a supportive environment where experiences, ideas, and knowledge are shared freely. Through our continuous efforts, we strive to make your graduation years fulfilling and memorable, ensuring you stay ahead in your academic journey while being a part of something larger—a community that values collaboration, accessibility, and mutual growth.

At DUaid, we believe that education is not just about individual achievement but about building a space where everyone can thrive together. Thank you for being a part of this journey with us. Together, let’s redefine what it means to succeed at Delhi University.
