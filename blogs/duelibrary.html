Delhi University E-Library



The University of Delhi has revolutionized learning by offering students, faculty, and researchers access to a treasure trove of e-resources through its advanced E-Library portal. Available 24/7, this initiative aims to cater to the diverse academic and research needs of its users with seamless access to resources from anywhere in the world.

The E-Library provides access to millions of digital resources, including over 25,000 e-journals and a staggering 88,000 e-books, covering a wide range of disciplines and fields. It empowers students and researchers by breaking geographical and time barriers, allowing them to access quality material at their convenience.

How to Access the E-Library

To access the resources, users need to log in with their institutional email ID and password at the official E-Library portal: https://duelibrary.in. The login process ensures secure and exclusive access for authorized users.

Dr. Rajesh Singh, the University Librarian, highlighted the importance of this platform, stating, "This E-Library is a step forward in making education more inclusive and resource-rich. It supports our vision of making quality education accessible to all members of the DU community."

For queries or support, users can contact the University Librarian, Dr. Rajesh Singh, at librarian@du.ac.in, or visit the DU library helpdesk. The university encourages students to make the most of this digital platform to excel in their academic pursuits.
